=====================================
  PLACEHOLDER FOR YOUR LOGO
=====================================

To add your logo:

1. Save your logo as: logo.png
2. Place it in: /public/logo.png
3. Requirements:
   - Format: PNG (recommended)
   - Size: 512x512px (square)
   - Background: Transparent
   - File size: Under 1MB

4. Your logo will automatically appear on the login page!

Current branding config: /public/branding.json
